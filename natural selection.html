Natural resources

Natural resources: refer to materials or substances occurring in environment and which can be exploited for economic gain.

Non-renewable natural resources:
is a resource of economic value that cannot be readily replaced by natural means on a level equal to its consumption.

Examples:

  • fossil fuels
  • oil
  • coal and natural gas
Renewable natural resources
can or will be replenished naturally in the course of time.

Examples

  • soil
  • water
  • solar energy
  • air
  • plants

Importance of Natural resources

  • Water is used for irigation

  • Lakes and rivers are source of food, such as fish and for recreation

  • Trees are major source of tiber, construction materials and firewood

  • Some animals such as gorillas, and other wild animals key in tourism sector
